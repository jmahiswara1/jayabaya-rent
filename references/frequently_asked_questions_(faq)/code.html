Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking, requirements, payments, and our vehicle services.

calendar_month Booking

How far in advance should I book a car? expand_more

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure vehicle availability, especially during weekends and holidays. For peak seasons (like Eid or New Year), booking 1-2 weeks in advance is highly advisable to secure your preferred vehicle type.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation? expand_more

Yes, you can modify your booking details such as dates, car type, or add-ons up to 12 hours before the scheduled pickup time via our website or mobile app. Changes are subject to vehicle availability and may affect the total rental price.

What happens if I need to cancel? expand_more

Cancellations made 24 hours prior to pickup are fully refundable. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a one-day rental fee. Please check our detailed cancellation policy in your booking confirmation email for specific terms related to your reservation type.

assignment_ind Rental Requirements

What documents do I need to bring? expand_more

You will need a valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year), a valid ID card (KTP) or Passport, and a credit card in the main driver's name for the security deposit. International renters must present a valid International Driving Permit (IDP).

Is there a minimum age for renting? expand_more

Yes, the minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. There is no maximum age limit as long as you have a valid medical certificate if over 70.

payments Price & Payment

Is a security deposit required? expand_more

Yes, a refundable security deposit is required at pick-up. This amount is blocked on your credit card and will be released within 14 business days after the vehicle is returned in good condition.

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. Cash payments are also accepted at select counter locations but a credit card is still required for the deposit.

Are there hidden fees? expand_more

We pride ourselves on transparency. The price you see includes basic insurance, taxes, and unlimited mileage (for rentals over 3 days). Any optional extras like GPS, child seats, or additional insurance coverage will be clearly itemized before you pay.

commute Operations & Vehicle Info

Do you offer airport pickup? expand_more

Absolutely. We offer meet-and-greet services at all major airports in our service area. Please provide your flight details during booking so our staff can track your arrival and meet you at the gate.

Are fuel costs included? expand_more

No, fuel is not included in the base rental price. We operate on a "Full-to-Full" policy, meaning the car is provided with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank to avoid refueling charges.

Do you offer one-way rentals? expand_more

Yes, one-way rentals between our major city branches are available. A one-way fee may apply depending on the drop-off location and the duration of the rental.
